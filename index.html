Bitcoin Tracker
October 2017 — Python, jQuery/JavaScript

A simple interface for tracking the Bitcoin portfolios of my flatmate and I. When the respective balance (NZD) is green, this means the portfolio has made a net profit. Conversely, red represents a net loss.

We have since cashed out our Bitcoin, so it doesn't serve any current functional purpose.
Try it out here, although note the bitcoin balances are spoofed.

Beep!
November 2016 — Java

An Android app I created to develop the habit of checking your mirrors whilst driving. I developed Beep! in preperation for my restricted license test.

When enabled, it emits a high-pitched tone every 10 seconds, which reminds the user to check their mirrors. The tone and and time interval can be changed by the user.

You can find Beep! on the Google Play Store.

Source Code on GitHub

OraTaiao Archive
2016 onward — Python, jQuery/JavaScript

A site I maintain for OraTaiao: New Zealand Climate & Health Council.

They moved from WikiSpaces to NationBuilder, and wanted to have access to the webpages and files on the old site. I used the main site as a template, and wrote a script that inserts the old webpages into the new website design. What's notable about this site is the use of spaces in the URLs, making reading and sharing links easier, as many pages and files have long names.
